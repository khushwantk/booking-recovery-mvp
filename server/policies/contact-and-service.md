# Contact, care, and chat reference (demo airline)

## Customer care phone (India)
For booking changes, refunds status, baggage tracing, and general queries, passengers may call the **24×7 Tata Airways Care** line. Demo published number: **1800-200-1234** (toll-free within India; IVR may route to English/Hindi). International callers may use country-specific numbers listed on the airline website in production.

## Chat transcript and chat ID
When using the website **Recovery Copilot**, each conversation has a **Chat ID** (for example `cc-…`). If you need a human agent, **save the chat as PDF** from the copilot panel and quote the **Chat ID** so support can match your session. This MVP does not automatically open a ticket from chat alone.

## Escalation when self-service is not enough
If policy or your booking screen does not answer your question, or you need a documented exception (medical waiver, bereavement, group contract), contact care with your **PNR or booking reference**, **passenger name**, and **Chat ID** if the query started in copilot.

## Grievances and feedback
Service complaints (staff conduct, meal quality, delay handling) may be logged via the web grievance form or care line. Keep booking references and any photos or receipts. Response timelines follow internal SLA; regulatory consumer portals apply where mandated.

## Social and messaging (demo)
Official support may be available on verified social channels; do not share OTPs, card numbers, or full passport data in public messages.

## Fraud and phishing
The airline will never ask for your full card CVV by email or SMS for a routine change. Report suspicious links to care using the official number only.
