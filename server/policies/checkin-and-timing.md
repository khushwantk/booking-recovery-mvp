# Check-in and timing (generic India domestic demo)

## Web check-in
Web check-in typically opens 48 hours before departure and closes up to 60 minutes before scheduled departure (carrier-specific).

## Airport reporting
Passengers should arrive at the airport early enough for baggage drop and security screening. Domestic counters may close 45-60 minutes before departure.

## Add-ons near departure
Some ancillaries (baggage, meals, seats) have closing windows before departure. If online windows are closed, airport purchase may still be possible at higher rates and subject to availability.

## Operational exceptions
Cutoff times, counter acceptance, and ancillary availability may vary due to airport congestion, weather disruption, or aircraft change.
