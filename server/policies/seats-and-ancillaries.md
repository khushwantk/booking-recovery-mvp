# Seats and ancillaries (generic India domestic demo)

## Seat selection
Standard seat selection may be included on Flex fares. Saver fares may select paid seats during booking, web check-in, or at airport (subject to availability).

## Preferred and extra-legroom seats
Preferred front-row and extra-legroom seats are chargeable add-ons and remain subject to aircraft configuration and safety restrictions.

## Meals
Pre-booked meals are available on eligible flights. Online pre-booking usually offers better value than airport purchase and has a cutoff window before departure.

## Insurance and value add-ons
Travel insurance, cancellation protection, and other value add-ons may be selected at booking. Terms depend on the partner insurer and selected coverage.

## Upgrades
Upgrade offers (paid or bid-based) may be presented after booking when operationally available. Upgrade confirmation is not guaranteed until ticketed.
