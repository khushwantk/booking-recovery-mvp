# Booking flow FAQ and glossary (demo)

## Payment declined or pending
If payment fails at checkout, verify card limits, OTP completion, and network. Retry once; duplicate charges are normally auto-reversed by the bank within standard cycles. If the amount is debited but no ticket is issued, contact care with bank reference and **Chat ID** from copilot if applicable.

## Itinerary and confirmation
After successful payment, an itinerary is emailed to the address on file. You may also download from Manage Booking. Demo environment may not send real email.

## Name spelling and minor corrections
Small spelling corrections (1–2 characters) may be allowed without fee on some fares; material name changes can require cancellation/rebook or fee per fare rules. Contact care with ID proof for review.

## Infant and child
Infants (under 2) typically travel on a guardian ticket with limited seat allocation rules. Children (2–11) require a child fare seat. Exact age rules and documentation are published at booking time.

## Group bookings
Ten or more passengers may qualify for group desk pricing and deposit rules. Online self-service may not show group inventory; use the group sales channel.

## Schedule change by airline
If the airline changes your flight time materially, rebooking or refund options may be offered per regulation and fare rule. Check SMS/email and Manage Booking.

## Weather and disruption
During widespread disruption, change fees may be waived temporarily; refundability still follows underlying fare class. Follow airport announcements and app alerts.

## Fare types in this demo
**Saver** — lower base fare; stricter change/refund rules. **Flex** — higher base fare; typically more flexible changes and refundable where stated.

## Miles and loyalty (demo)
Frequent-flyer accrual and redemption are subject to programme rules, partner airlines, and booking class. Retro-credit may require boarding pass and ticket copy within published windows.

## Accessibility and special assistance
Wheelchair assistance, visual/hearing assistance, and dietary medical meals should be requested at booking or as early as possible (minimum hours before departure apply). Airport day-of requests are subject to capacity.

## Dangerous goods and pets
Restricted articles must not be carried in cabin or checked baggage except as per IATA rules. Pet carriage is route- and aircraft-dependent; advance booking required.
