# Baggage policy (generic India domestic demo)

## Cabin baggage
Each passenger may carry one cabin bag up to 7 kg and one small personal item (laptop bag, handbag, or small backpack) that fits under the seat.

## Checked baggage (pre-booked)
Economy passengers may buy checked baggage during booking or in Manage Booking. Standard allowance for this demo is up to 23 kg per bag, subject to route and fare rules.

## Extra baggage at airport counter
Passengers may add extra checked baggage at the airport check-in counter, subject to space, safety, and cutoff-time limits. Airport rates are generally higher than pre-booked online rates.

## Excess, oversize, and special items
Bags above weight or size limits incur excess baggage fees. Sports equipment (such as golf bags and bicycles) may require advance declaration and handling charges.

## Cutoff guidance
For best pricing and smoother check-in, baggage should be added before web check-in closes. Final acceptance of baggage at airport remains operationally subject to staff approval and flight load.

## Lost, delayed, or damaged baggage
Report missing or damaged bags immediately at the airport baggage desk and obtain a Property Irregularity Report (PIR). Claims for interim expenses and repair/replacement follow carrier and Montreal Convention limits where applicable; keep baggage tags and receipts.

## Medical and mobility equipment
Battery-powered wheelchairs, CPAP devices, and other assistive items may require advance notice, safety documentation, and disassembly for carriage. Contact the airline before travel day.
